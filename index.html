scr33n - Chromebook Unenroll Exploit

This exploit was discovered while I was messing around in Dev Mode. When I tried to boot back into normal mode, I accidentally pressed the refresh and power buttons simultaneously. To my surprise, after letting it load, the device entered a kiosk-like app controlled by Google.

This exploit allows you to unenroll a Chromebook by following these steps:

  1. Powerwash your device: Reset your Chromebook to its factory settings.
  2. EC Reset during enrollment: While your device is enrolling, perform an EC reset.
  3. Restart and leave it: After the reset, let your device restart and leave it alone.

You should end up in a kiosk-like app, but it will be Google.
